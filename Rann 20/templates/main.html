ABOUT RANN
RANN The National Level Sports Fest of KIET Group of Institutions is a three days mega event with participants from more than 70 reputed colleges.

It is aimed at leading by example of sportsmanship. Rann infuses passion in veins and emboldens the action being performed.

MESSAGE
Dr.(Col) A Garg (Director)

Swami Vivekananda once said “We want that education by which character is formed, strength of mind is increased, the intellect is expanded and by which one can stand on one’s own feet”. KIET GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS is tirelessly devoted to the cause of providing quality legal education to its students. Following the Motto of Dharma Sansthapanartham (for the sake of establishing the primacy of the laws of eternal values), every student in KIET GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS is not only encouraged to persist enthusiastically and vigorously towards their AKTU Academics but also towards an all-encompassing/ inclusive quest for knowledge and wisdom.
It is said that a good work-life balance is key for an individual’s happiness in today’s fast paced world. It is my most humble opinion while academic pursuits certainly contribute towards success, character building and holistic development can be achieved only by participating in a variety of activities to delight/ entice the myriad senses of the human mind.
Sports not only contributes towards physical and mental well-being in the age of computers & electronics, but also fosters a sense of independence, cooperation and team involvement. Perhaps, most importantly, extra-curricular activities teach students the importance of participation and involvement thereby imprinting wonderful experiences and memories.
It gives me immense pleasure to introduce the 5 th Edition of KIET GROUP OF INSTITUTION’S Annual Sports Fest, RANN’2020. Certainly, just as all our prior editions were a resounding success, I have no doubt that this edition will be equally fulfilling and enjoyable for the participants. I wish all the participants and my students the very best.
